my story

Every successful journey starts with passion.

Download resume arrow

I am cerently living in Kiev Ukrain.
At the age of 23, I was introduced to user experience design and was surprised by how much this profession resonates with my passions and interests in understanding human behavior, perception, cognition, and how people interact with the world.

I managed to work in several of the largest companies in Ukraine, participate in a startup, worked for a world leader in eye tracking and develop applications for windows.

Now my focus for the future is on exploring Extended reality (XR), the possibilities and limitations of this technology, and making it available for mass adoption.
